Project Overview

This project was completed as part of a job simulation focused on robotics and control systems. The objective was to analyze inefficiencies in robotic arm performance and optimize system responsiveness. By leveraging Python-based diagnostic tools, system behavior was evaluated and improved through iterative simulation techniques.
